Phase 1 — Months 0 to 12

Operational Takeover &
Luxury Transformation

Management

  • Day to day management and operations of the Sunset Beach Resort & Residence.
  • Establishing operational policies and Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)
  • Design and execute a new commercial and marketing strategy to ensure optimal reach to targeted segments.
  • Strengthen Online presence and overall visibility: Hotel or brand website, Online Booking Integration, social media, etc…

  • Organize the operations staffing structure and overall framework.
  • Evaluate all costs and expenses, take necessary actions to reduce them without affecting the overall service quality.

Consultancy Services

  • Development of the future strategic concept and positioning that reflect the project's identity and meet market needs.
  • Built a Business Plan according to the desired concept approach.
  • Facilitate negotiations with International Brands.
  • Built an Area Program, respecting International standards and/or selected brand requirements.
  • Provide our expertise into giving you the appropriate recommendations for development management, design review, project management, and cost control during this phase.

Phase 2 — Months 13 to 36

Design, Technical Services &
Operational Integration

Working alongside your design teams, we will:

Master Plan & Architectural Review

  • Review master plans, site plans and circulation (guest, staff, logistics, services).
  • Ensure efficient zoning of rooms, public areas, aqua park, residences, BOH, parking and access.

Configuration & Space Planning

  • Confirm and fine-tune the number of keys and their mix (standard rooms, suites, special units, branded residences).
  • Optimize public areas, F&B outlets, meeting rooms, spa, aqua park and kids’ areas in line with target segments and revenue strategy.
  • Validate and improve BOH areas (receiving, storage, kitchens, laundry, staff facilities) for operational efficiency.

Interior Design Integration

  • Review interior design concepts and layouts for rooms, corridors, F&B, meeting rooms and public spaces.
  • Ensure that functional, emotional and brand requirements are all met.
  • Advise on signage concepts (external and internal) that support navigation, safety and commercial flows.

Guest Journey Flows & Experience Blueprints

  • Map physical and emotional guest flows: arrivals, circulation to rooms, F&B, wellness, kids’ activities, etc…
  • Identify friction points and re-design them into opportunities for delight and revenue (e.g. upgrade moments, cross-sell touchpoints, intuitive signposting toward high-margin venues).
  • Develop experience blueprints that link design, staffing, SOPs and technology to the desired emotional outcome and commercial KPIs (ADR, TRevPAR, upsell conversion, repeat and referral rates).

Food & Beverage Infrastructure Design

  • Review and optimize layouts for restaurants, bars, cafés, banqueting and support areas.
  • Define preliminary F&B equipment lists and functional zoning (kitchens, cold rooms, bars, counters, dishwashing, storage).
  • Support evaluation and recommendation of key F&B equipment and suppliers.

Information Technology & Systems

  • Advise on the hotel’s IT backbone, including PMS, POS, reservations and distribution connectivity.
  • Review requirements for network, back-office systems, telephony, CCTV, key systems, TV, payment solutions and guest internet.
  • Ensure AV systems for meeting rooms and event spaces are aligned with the market positioning and revenue plan.

Construction Support & Project Monitoring

  • Provide technical assistance visits at key stages.
  • Review detailed drawings and design modifications for operational and brand compliance.
  • Flag risks early and propose corrective actions.

Phase 2 — Months 13 to 36

PreOpening Preparation, Guest Experience
& Commercial Launch

We will support the project through pre-opening and launch by focusing on:

Pre-Opening Project Management

  • Develop pre-opening critical paths and milestone plans.
  • Coordinate with project stakeholders.
  • Monitor readiness and manage risk items.

Organization & Staffing

  • Organizational structures and key positions
  • Defining job profiles and recruitment priorities.

Commercial Strategy & Launch Plan

  • Set up a residential sales roadmap (lead generation, events, site-visits, closing) and a resort launch plan (pricing, distribution, partnerships).
  • Link marketing and digital strategies to specific KPIs (direct booking ratio, unit sales, upsell ratios, campaign ROIs).

Guest Experience, Service Design & Culture Activation

  • Create concrete guest journeys and service scenarios for each segment
  • Design service rituals and interaction techniques at key revenue moments.
  • Use the Learn by Being™ model to train leaders and teams on the floor.
  • Deploy GX tools to monitor guest and team culture and relate these metrics to revenue KPIs (ADR, TRevPAR, upsell conversion, repeat and referral rates).

Pre-Opening Readiness Audits

  • Conduct readiness checks for rooms, public areas, systems and teams.
  • Provide final recommendations and action plans prior to soft opening.

Phase 3 — 15 Years

Full Management &
Brand Guardianship

Long-term operational management under a Hotel Management Agreement, with continuous luxury quality assurance and brand guardianship ensuring Sunset Beach maintains its position as the Eastern Province's premier destination.

Hotel Management

  • Full operational management per HMA terms
  • Continuous performance optimization
  • Revenue management & cost control
  • Ongoing staff training & talent development
  • Technology & systems evolution

Luxury Quality Assurance

  • Ongoing brand guardianship & standards monitoring
  • Periodic luxury service training refreshers
  • Guest experience audits & improvement programs
  • Reputation management & KPI tracking
  • F&B concept evolution & lifestyle curation

Contract Term: 15 years initial term, renewable for an additional 5 years upon mutual consent. Management fees structured as Base Fee (% of Gross Revenue) plus Incentive Fee (% of Adjusted Gross Operating Profit), aligning operator performance with owner returns.
